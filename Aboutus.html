E-commerce

"Ecommerce" or "electronic commerce" is the trading of goods and services on the internet. Nowadays people tend to buy everything online so that they do not have to wait in long lines at cash registers to pay for the products they have purchased. They can shop comfortably of their own home or workplace, saving time on transportation.

This gives them an idea how to make online shopping more convenient. It is a search engine that shoppers use to filter and compare products based on price, features, reviews and other criteria.

The E-commerce Product Search and Price Comparison project is a web-based application that allows users to search for products across different online retailers, compare prices, and find the best deals. The application leverages an E-commerce API, which provides access to product information and pricing from various e-commerce platforms. The primary goal of this project is to help users make informed purchasing decisions by presenting them with a comprehensive view of product options and prices from multiple sources.
